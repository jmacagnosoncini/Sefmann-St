Our Design Process: step-by-step

1. Initial Consultation

We begin by understanding your needs, preferences, and goals for the space. This meeting is an opportunity for us to ask questions and gather as much detail as possible to craft a design tailored to your vision.

2. Concept Development

Based on our discussion, we create initial design concepts, mood boards, and layouts that align with your style, functionality, and budget. This stage includes reviewing different materials, color schemes, and furniture choices.

3. Design Refinement

After reviewing the concept, we refine the design based on your feedback. This may include revisiting color choices, layouts, or suggesting different options until we achieve a final vision that excites you.

4. Implementation

Once the design is approved, we move into the implementation phase. We collaborate with trusted contractors and suppliers to bring the design to life, handling everything from sourcing furniture and materials to overseeing construction or installation.

5. Project Handover

After completing the installation, we walk through the space with you to ensure everything is in place and meets your expectations. Any final tweaks are made, and we provide you with advice on how to care for the new space.
